## IBM Cloud Foundation Skills Series Videos

The following videos help enable you to get familiar with the foundation of the IBM Cloud Platform

![Cloud Platform](./images/overview.png)

The foundation series has been created by James Belton, IBM Cloud Adoption Leader. James has create a very useful
set of videos that I would encourage you to work through.

To access the videos navigate the video sub category in the menu and you will see a video tile. Select the
tile and it will display the video for you to watch inline in the same browser session. To remove the playing
video click outside of the video area on the page and it will be removed from display.a
